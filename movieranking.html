THE BEST MOVIES I SAW

My top 3 movies favorites of all-time.

Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse

Like the work of a young artist who refuses to be restrained by the borders of the frame, “Across the Spider-Verse” is loaded with incredible imagery and fascinating ideas.

Victim/Suspect

“Victim/Suspect” has a guiding light for truth in the form of rising journalist Rachel de Leon, who works at the Center for Investigative Reporting.

Fast X

a film that brazenly plays like a Greatest Hits collection from a hit artist.
